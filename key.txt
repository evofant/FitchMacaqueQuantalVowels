F1 = first formant in synthesized vowel sound
F2 = second formant in synthesized vowel sound
f0 = fundamental frequency in synthesized vowel sound

The resulting vowel space does not include quantal vowel 
the most common phonemes observed across human spoken 
languages (Maddieson, 1984; Moran & McCloy, 2019; Stevens, 
1989; see also Liljencrants & Lindblom, 1972). 

For macaque vowels, correspondence to human vowel is 
approximate.
